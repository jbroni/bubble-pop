Level 1
    How to play
    👆
    Goal. Tap a group of 2 or more touching same-color blobs to pop them (costs 1 move). Tapping a lone blob just wiggles it — free. Clear the target color or hit the target score before you run out of moves.
    ⭐
    Scoring. Popping a group of size n scores n² × 5 points. Bigger groups score way more than the same blobs popped separately.
    🧨
    Bomb. Pop 5 blobs at once to create one — tap it to blast every blob in its 3×3 neighborhood, any color (n × 50 points).
    🚀
    Rocket. Pop 6–7 blobs at once to create one — tap it to clear its whole row or column (n × 70 points).
    🌈
    Rainbow. Pop 8+ blobs at once to create one — tap it to clear every blob of the board's most common color (n × 90 points).
    🏆
    Stars. Clear a level with 28% or more of your moves left for 3★, 10% or more for 2★, otherwise 1★.
